## Iteration 69 - US-082: Free time finder
- What was implemented: Created `rex/calendar_backends/free_time_finder.py` with `TimeSlot` dataclass and `find_free_slots()`. Algorithm walks day-by-day, clips to working hours, merges/sorts busy blocks, then steps through free gaps emitting slots. Exported from `rex/calendar_backends/__init__.py`. 32 tests in `tests/test_us082_free_time_finder.py`.
- Files changed: rex/calendar_backends/free_time_finder.py (new), rex/calendar_backends/__init__.py, tests/test_us082_free_time_finder.py (new), PRD.md, progress.txt
- Learnings for future iterations:
  - `find_free_slots(busy_blocks, range_start, range_end, duration_minutes, ...)` signature
  - `day_start_hour`/`day_end_hour` (default 9/18) clip each calendar day to working hours
  - `slot_step_minutes` (default 30) controls how densely slots are packed within each gap
  - `max_slots` stops early once N slots collected (useful for pagination)
  - Algorithm: for each gap between busy blocks (clipped to window), step from gap_start to gap_end-duration, yield TimeSlot per step
  - `_ensure_utc()` copied locally to avoid circular import from free_busy_stub
